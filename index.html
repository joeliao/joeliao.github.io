Disclaimer

To submit an issue to the City, please go to: http://www.seattle.gov/customer-service-bureau or call 206-684-ROAD.

Privacy Agreenment

Information Collected and How it is Used
The City of Seattle gathers certain information automatically when you visit the Seattle.Gov web site,such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, domain name, browser and operating system types, date and time of site visit, web services or pages visited, and web site visited prior to visiting our web site. This information is collected for statistical purposes only to help the City of Seattle manage the Seattle.Gov web site.

Except for authorized law enforcement investigations and web site security purposes, no other attempts are made to identify individual users.

Personal Information
We collect no personal informationabout you unless you voluntarily provide it to us by sending us an email, participating in a survey, hosted mailing-list, or discussion board, completing an online form, or doing some other type of online transaction on Seattle.Gov and with the City of Seattle.

Information provided for a transaction is used only for completing and recording the transaction.

The City does not sell, rent or give out visitor information, including e-mail addresses, to any outside company or organization, unless legally required to do so.

Public Access to Information
The Public Records Act requires the disclosure of all public records unless a particular record is specifically exempt under the Public Records Act or other applicable law.

Use of Cookies
The City's policy is to limit the use of cookies, but some cookies are used to confirm the integrity of online transactions. Cookies used in this way do not contain personally identifiable information.

Contact Information
You can contact a City staff member to review any personally identifiable information we collect about you by using the Contact Information section of the full Online Privacy and Security web site at http://www.seattle.gov/pan/privacypol.htm.

If you have questions about this document, call the Dept of Information Technology at 206-684-0600. We will provide assistance in your own language with an interpreter upon request.

Security
The City safeguards the integrity of its data and prevents unauthorized access to information it maintains.

Electronic Commerce
Electronic payment transactions are allowed only where there are technological and other safeguards in place to protect financial and other sensitive data.

Avoiding Internet Fraud
It is the City's policy never to request confidential personal or financial information from our customers via an unsolicited e-mail, or to send you an unsolicited e-mail containing a link to a City website where confidential personal or financial information is requested.

Other Websites
When you link to another web site, you are no longer on the City's web site and this Privacy Notice will not apply.

Important:
  • Recommendations cannot be deleted once submitted, see the "Notes" section for more details.
Add a recommendation:
  • Zoom to an area of interest on the map
  • Click the notepad/pen icon in the upper-right corner
  • Select a Public Input Category using the drop down menu
  • Complete the other 4 questions by typing into the boxes
  • On the map, click the exact recommendation location
  • Click the “Submit Recommendation” button
  • Or click the X button to cancel and not submit the recommendation
  • Repeat these steps to add more recommendations
Add comments to other recommendations:
  • Click the Comments icon on a recommendation
  • Enter a comment in the pop up box
  • Click the plus button to submit the comment
Notes:
  • Recommendation locations cannot be edited or deleted once the “Submit Recommendations” button is clicked by the user. Please add a note in the comments if the location should be removed by the SDOT Administrator.
Keyboard shortcuts:
  • "Click" hold & drag to pan
  • "Shift-click" & draw rectangle to zoom in
  • "Ctrl-shift-click" & draw rectangle to zoom out
  • "Mouse wheel" & roll forward to zoom in
  • "Mouse wheel" & roll backward to zoom out
